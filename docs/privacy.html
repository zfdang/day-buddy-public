Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Day Buddy ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your data and respects your rights.

2. Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal data on our servers.

Day Buddy is a local-first application. All your events, reminders, and settings are stored locally on your device using an internal database.

3. Cloud Backup (WebDAV)

You may verify the "Data Safety" section in Google Play. We declare that we do not collect user data.

However, the app offers an optional WebDAV Backup feature. If you explicitly enable and configure this feature:

  • Your data (database backup) is encrypted and uploaded to your own private WebDAV server (e.g., Nextcloud, ownCloud, or other providers).
  • We (the developer) have no access to your WebDAV credentials or the files stored on your server.
  • Your WebDAV password is securely stored on your device using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences and is never transmitted to us.

4. Device Permissions

To function correctly, Day Buddy requests the following permissions:

  • Alarms & Reminders (SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM): Required to send you precise notifications for your scheduled events.
  • Boot Completed: Required to reschedule your reminders automatically after you restart your device.
  • Notifications: Required to display alerts in your system notification tray.
  • Internet: Required only if you use the WebDAV backup feature to connect to your specified server.
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during notifications.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your local data. Specifically, sensitive information such as your WebDAV password is encrypted using the Android Jetpack Security library.

6. Children's Privacy

Our application does not knowingly collect or solicit personal information from anyone under the age of 13. As the app functions offline and does not collect data, it is safe for all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at zfdang@gmail.com.

Why DayBuddy?

Your reliable assistant for managing life's recurring events.

Birthdays & Anniversaries

Never forget a birthday or anniversary again. Set yearly reminders and get notified in advance so you have time to prepare.

Bills & Payments

Track monthly utility bills, credit card payments, rent, and subscriptions. Stay on top of your finances with timely reminders.

Lunar Calendar Support

Perfect for Chinese traditional holidays and lunar birthdays. Set events like "Lunar New Year" or "Grandma's birthday on Lunar March 15th" with ease.

Flexible Recurrence

Daily, weekly, monthly, yearly — or custom patterns like "every 2 weeks" or "last Friday of each month". Handle any schedule with confidence.

Period Tracking

A bonus feature for women: intelligent menstrual cycle tracking with predictions for period, fertile, and safe windows.

Privacy First

All your data stays on your device. No cloud uploads, no tracking, no accounts required. Your personal information remains private.

Secure Cloud Backup

Optional WebDAV backup to your own server. Your data is encrypted locally before upload, ensuring that only you can access it.
